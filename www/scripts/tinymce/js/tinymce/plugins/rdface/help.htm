RDFaCE (RDFa Content Editor)


RDFaCE is a Semantic content editor based on TinyMCE WYSIWYG editor. RDFaCE is created as a proof of concept for WYSIWYM (What You See Is What You Mean) concept. WYSIWYM aims to enable end-users to easily annotate their content using RDFa and Microdata markups. RDFaCE employs external NLP APIs to suggest namespaces, properties, URIs and to automatically annotate content.
RDFaCE is maintained by Agile Knowledge Engineering and Semantic Web (AKSW) Research Group, University of Leipzig.
More information about RDFaCE can be found in RDFaCE project page.
